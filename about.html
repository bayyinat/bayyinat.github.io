About Bayyinat

Discover our story, our mission, and the people who make Bayyinat special.

Who We Are

Bayyinat is dedicated to bringing the love of the Qur’an and the Arabic language into the hearts of learners worldwide. We believe in a playful, kid-friendly approach that inspires curiosity, cultivates confidence, and creates lifelong bonds with Islamic knowledge.

Our Mission

To cultivate a deep appreciation for the Holy Qur’an and its language among children and adults alike, through engaging lessons, certified teachers, and a supportive, interactive environment.

Our Vision

A world where every child feels connected to the Qur’an and confident in the Arabic language, fostering a generation of spiritually enriched, compassionate, and knowledgeable communities.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat every student with kindness and respect.
  • Curiosity: We encourage learners to ask questions and explore.
  • Community: We strive to build supportive relationships and shared growth.
  • Excellence: We continuously refine our teaching methods for the best outcomes.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of passionate educators, Bayyinat set out with a clear goal: to revolutionize Quranic and Arabic learning for a global audience. Initially starting with small, local workshops, we quickly expanded online to reach students worldwide. Today, Bayyinat is proud to serve families across different continents, providing interactive lessons and bridging cultural gaps through the universal language of the Qur’an.

Milestones

2023

Launched Bayyinat with a handful of certified teachers and a vision for fun, interactive lessons.

2024

2025+

Continuing our journey to make Qur’an and Arabic learning accessible to families everywhere.
