About Bayyinat

Discover our story, our mission, and the people who make Bayyinat special.

Who We Are

Bayyinat is dedicated to bringing the love of the Qur’an and the Arabic language into the hearts of learners worldwide. We believe in a playful, kid-friendly approach that inspires curiosity and lasting knowledge.

Our Mission

To cultivate a deep appreciation for the Holy Qur’an and its language among children and adults alike, through engaging lessons, certified teachers, and a supportive learning environment.

Our Vision

A world where every child feels connected to the Qur’an and confident in the Arabic language, fostering a generation of lifelong learners and spiritually enriched communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Bayyinat started as a small group of passionate educators who saw a need for a fun, interactive way to teach Quranic studies and Arabic. Over the years, we have grown into an online platform that reaches students globally, staying true to our core values of compassion, curiosity, and community.
